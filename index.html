⚽ How to Play
🎯 The goal
You're shown a real starting XI on a pitch — just the position and how many letters in each surname. Guess every player to complete the lineup.
💯 Scoring
Each player is worth up to 100 points. Using a hint reduces what that player is worth — guess them with no hints for full points.
💡 Hints
Reveal Nationality/Club (−40pts), Reveal a Letter (−20 to −40pts depending how many you've used), or Reveal Player (−150pts, for when you're stuck).
🟢 Easy / Hard mode
Tap any player in Easy mode for a free hint (their club or nationality) — no points lost. Hard mode gives no free hints, so your score reflects your knowledge alone.
🔥 Daily Lineup & streaks
A new puzzle every day. Complete it on consecutive days to build a streak — miss a day and it resets.
